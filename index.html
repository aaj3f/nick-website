Nicholas Wolters is Assistant Professor of Spanish in the Department of Spanish & Italian. Before joining the faculty at Wake Forest, he earned his MA in Spanish and French from the University of Delaware (2011) and completed his PhD in Spanish at the University of Virginia (2016). His teaching and research interests include modern Spanish peninsular literature and visual cultures (18th-21st centuries), Catalan studies, gender theory, and television studies.
